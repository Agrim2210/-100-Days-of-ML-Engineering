# Insights — Data Leakage

Data leakage can lead to highly misleading model performance.

Models trained with leaked information may appear extremely accurate during evaluation but fail when deployed.

**Our Experiment also demonstrated that how model accuracy become very large with data leakage and after cleaning ,accuracy become less.**

Identifying leakage requires understanding the timing and availability of each feature.

Proper data splitting and preprocessing practices are essential to prevent leakage.

Avoiding leakage is critical for building trustworthy machine learning systems.
