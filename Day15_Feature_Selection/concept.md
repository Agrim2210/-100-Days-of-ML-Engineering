# Mathematical Foundations — Feature Selection

## Filter Methods

Filter methods use statistical measures to evaluate
the relationship between features and the target variable.

Example:

Correlation measures linear relationship strength.

---

## SelectKBest

SelectKBest selects the top K features based on
statistical tests.

For regression:
f_regression measures dependency between feature
and target.

---

## Key Idea

Feature selection reduces dimensionality and helps
models focus on the most informative variables.
