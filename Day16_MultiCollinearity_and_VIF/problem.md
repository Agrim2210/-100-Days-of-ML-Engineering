# Day 16 — Multicollinearity and VIF

## Problem

In datasets with highly correlated features, machine learning models,
especially linear models, may become unstable.

Even though model accuracy may remain high, the coefficients can become unreliable and difficult to interpret.

## Objective

This experiment investigates multicollinearity using:

• Correlation matrix  
• Variance Inflation Factor (VIF)  

It also demonstrates how removing correlated features improves model stability and interpretability.
