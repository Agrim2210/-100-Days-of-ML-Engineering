# Mathematical Foundations — ROC Curve

## True Positive Rate

TPR = TP / (TP + FN)

This metric is also known as recall or sensitivity.

---

## False Positive Rate

FPR = FP / (FP + TN)

It measures how many negative samples were incorrectly
classified as positive.

---

## ROC Curve

The ROC curve plots

True Positive Rate (TPR)
against
False Positive Rate (FPR)

for different classification thresholds.

---

## Area Under Curve

AUC represents the area under the ROC curve.

Interpretation:

AUC ≈ probability that a randomly chosen positive sample
is ranked higher than a randomly chosen negative sample.
